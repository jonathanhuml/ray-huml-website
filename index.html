About

Raymond A. Huml provides independent consulting for companies and organizations working in biopharmaceutical development. The practice is built for thoughtful, senior-level support: clear analysis, practical recommendations, and direct communication.

Huml Biopharma Consulting LLC works with clients who need an experienced outside perspective on development strategy, program planning, scientific positioning, and the many decisions that sit between promising science and durable execution.
